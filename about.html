// Services

What We Do

Six outcome-defined services. Each delivered as a fixed-scope engagement with measurable results.

01 // Operational Excellence aaS

Make your IT operations 20% more efficient.

IT landscape assessment, process harmonization, task analysis with automation scoring, and board-ready recommendations.
02 // Savings aaS

Recover 10–40% of your IT vendor spend in 4–6 weeks.

AI contract analysis, vendor-by-vendor savings report, benchmarking vs €3B+ database, and prioritized renegotiation roadmap.
03 // Vendor Consolidation aaS

Reduce dozens of vendors to a handful of strategic partners.

Sourcing location identifier, make vs buy vs partner model, ITSM ticket analysis, and target supplier architecture.
04 // Complex RFx aaS

Run fair, structured IT tenders in 6–8 weeks.

Requirements engineering, RFI & market analysis, complex RFP with ZVD methodology, and weighted vendor evaluation.
05 // Vendor Management aaS

Hold your IT vendors accountable to what they signed.

Sourcing implementation guide, frame contract design, 3-tier governance framework, KPI framework & financial management.
06 // managedsuppliers

Manage every vendor in one SaaS platform.

Aventario's consulting frameworks turned into software you run yourself — permanent organizational capability.

// Values

Our Values

01

Honesty over comfort

We tell clients what we find, not what they want to hear. 25 years in this industry means we've seen what happens when that stops.

02

Pragmatism — listen, cut, deliver

We skip the theory. If a process can be standardized, we standardize it. If a vendor is underperforming, we say so — then we fix it.

03

Defined outcomes, not open scopes

Every engagement starts with a clear, measurable result. Not a deliverable list — a number. We hold ourselves to it.

04

Accountability — we keep our word

Outcomes are defined upfront. If we committed to a 20% efficiency gain, that's what you get — or we haven't finished.

05

Capability, not dependency

We work alongside your team and transfer the methodology as we go. When we leave, the result stays.

06

Continuous improvement

We improve our own methods the same way we improve yours — systematically, based on data, and without ego.
