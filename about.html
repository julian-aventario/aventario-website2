Why this way.

The classic consulting model is paid for the analysis. The classic supplier model is paid for the hours. Both reward more activity, not better outcomes.

We sized our model differently. Quantified outcomes. Fixed scope. Senior people in the room. Hand-off only when the structure stands on its own.

It means we say no more often than most. It also means clients get what we promised them.
