// The Problem

Good governance processes die when people leave.

Even organizations that invest in building proper vendor governance frameworks eventually lose them. The processes live in Excel spreadsheets, email threads, SharePoint folders, and people's heads. When key people leave, governance degrades. When priorities shift, vendor reviews get skipped.

74% of organizations don't use dedicated contract lifecycle management tools. The result: vendor management is a perpetual consulting project, not a permanent organizational capability.

"What CRM do you use to manage customer relationships? Can you imagine running sales without it? Now ask: what do you use to manage supplier relationships?"

// Platform Features

Built by practitioners. For practitioners.

SOW Creation & Management

Templates, role-based collaboration, real-time co-editing, frame contract reuse, change tracking, and reviewer workflows. Every statement of work, structured from day one.

Multi-Vendor Negotiation

Side-by-side supplier comparison, weighted scoring, change tracking, time-to-agree metrics, and DocuSign integration. Negotiation managed as a structured process, not an email chain.

Real-Time KPI Measurement

KPIs linked directly to SOWs. Real-time dashboards, compliance tracking, financial tracking, SLC monitoring. Know whether vendors are delivering — before the quarterly meeting.

Improvement Management

Centralized improvement hub, task assignment, status tracking, category-based organization, and full audit trail. Performance issues tracked through to resolution — not just documented.

Financial Forecasting

Actual vs. planned analysis, scenario modeling, and deviation alerts. Understand your vendor spend trajectory — not just your current invoices.

Change Request Lifecycle

CR management from within the SOW, with version control, audit trail, signature integration, and automatic contract updates. No more change requests that disappear into email.
