How we approach it

We start with how the work flows today, not how the org chart says it flows. Senior advisors sit in with the teams running the processes, the AI tooling does the volume on tickets, logs and time-tracking data.

From there we build the case for change. Specific tasks. Specific automation candidates. Specific operating-model adjustments. Sized to what your organization can actually absorb in the next twelve months.
