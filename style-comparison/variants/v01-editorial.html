Section B · The Method essay ii

Spark to turnkey. We own the result.

The classic consulting model is paid for the analysis. The classic supplier model is paid for the hours. Both reward more activity, not better outcomes.

We see ourselves as a full-service provider, supporting your initiatives from the initial spark through to "turnkey" completion. We drive sustainable results by engaging and empowering the whole affected organisation — its people, processes and stakeholders — throughout every phase.

Every project is staffed against our three delivery pillars, so you get one seamless, integrated service rather than a sequence of hand-offs. The team that scopes the engagement runs it through to the amendment. We don't hand over a roadmap and leave: our teams sit inside the programme until structures, processes and supplier relationships stand on their own.
